Criminal Law

Specialized representation in criminal defense, ensuring justice is upheld for all clients.

✔Common offenses include theft, fraud, assault, drug-related crimes, and violent crimes like murder.

Child Support

Comprehensive support to ensure children receive fair financial and emotional care post-divorce or separation.

✔A legal obligation for a non-custodial parent to provide financial support for their child.
✔Covers expenses like food, housing, education, and medical care.
✔Calculated based on income, custody arrangements, and the child’s needs.
✔Can be modified if circumstances change, like job loss or increased costs.
✔Typically required until the child turns 18 or finishes school

Business Law

Business Law is a branch of law that governs the legal rights, relationships, and conduct of individuals and businesses engaged in commerce and trade. It covers a broad range of areas, including contracts, corporate governance, intellectual property, and employment law. Business law ensures that businesses comply with local, state, and federal regulations, helping companies manage risk and avoid legal disputes. It also provides mechanisms for resolving conflicts, such as through litigation or alternative dispute resolution methods. Key aspects include business formation, mergers and acquisitions, taxation, and bankruptcy. Overall, business law is crucial for protecting business interests, maintaining fair practices, and ensuring smooth operations in a competitive marketplace.
